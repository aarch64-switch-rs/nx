AppletResourceUserId
AppletResourceUserIds are restricted to the values of zero, or the process’ PID. When they are sent over an IPC interface, they also trigger the sending of a PID descriptor in the HIPC request, so there is an additional field for the PID. This field is filled in by the kernel during a request, and is read out of the headers in the same way as the ProcessIdProcessId above.
